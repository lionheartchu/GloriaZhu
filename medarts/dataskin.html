Data Visualization & Future Design

Data Skinware

2024

What has the digitalization brought us? While doing data visualization, we are being “visualized” as well.

Our vulnerable data, like a skinless skeleton, is exposed to cyber risks in a nude state. Using a “data skin” as a creative solution, the project Data Skinware envisions the future of data protection.

Research

The project began with research into data vulnerabilities, privacy concerns, and the psychological aspects of digital exposure. I have explored how different types of personal data correspond to different forms of vulnerability.
