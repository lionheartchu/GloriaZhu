Data "Skinware"

Creative research and exploration on how to use metaphor-driven design to visualize digital vulnerability.

Data "Skinware" examines how our vulnerable data can be conceptually protected, using immersive experiences to explore the relationship between digital identity and physical embodiment.

Research

The research explored how digital data vulnerability can be understood through physical metaphors, examining the concept of data as a form of "skin" that protects our digital identity.

Outcome

An immersive installation that visualizes data protection through interactive experiences, creating a space where visitors can explore their relationship with digital vulnerability and protection.
