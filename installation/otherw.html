Interactive Installation · 2024

Otherworld

Led by urbanization, the boundary between humans and nature gets blurred with unrestricted human activities that continuously exploit natural resources.

The two-part interactive project features enabling the perspective sharing between humans and plants through scale-switching, creating an immersive experience that blends physical and digital environments.

Research

The project began with exploring how human activities have led to urbanization, and the problems it has brought. Research focused on understanding the relationship between humans and nature in the context of modern urban environments.

Outcome

Using Kinect for body-sensing interaction, Otherworld creates a responsive, immersive experience that focuses on perspective-shifting and empathy with nature. The installation adapts to visitors, creating unique experiences based on their movement and interactions.
