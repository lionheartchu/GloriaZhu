Interactive Installation

Otherworld

2024

Led by urbanization, the boundary between humans and nature gets blurred with unrestricted human activities that continuously exploit natural resources.

The two-part interactive project features enabling the perspective sharing between humans and plants through scale-switching.

Research

The project began with exploring the relationship between human perception and virtual environments. We investigated how different sensory inputs could create immersive experiences.

Outcome

Otherworld creates a responsive environment where visitors become active participants rather than passive observers. The installation has been featured in multiple galleries and received recognition for its innovative approach to interactive art.
