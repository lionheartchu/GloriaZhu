Interactive Installation

YU

2021

Sound, space, and interaction converge.

YU is an interactive sound installation that explores the relationship between human movement and spatial acoustics, creating a dynamic auditory environment that responds to the presence and actions of visitors.

Challenge

Design an installation that transforms human movement into meaningful sound experiences.

Approach

A technical approach combining motion sensors, spatial audio, and custom software to create responsive soundscapes.

Outcome

An installation that generates unique sonic environments based on the movements and interactions of its visitors.

Research

The project began with research into spatial audio, movement tracking technologies, and the psychological impact of sound. We explored how different movements could be translated into meaningful sonic experiences.

Outcome

YU creates a responsive sonic environment where visitors' movements generate and modify soundscapes in real-time. The installation has been exhibited in multiple venues and has been recognized for its innovative approach to interactive sound art.
