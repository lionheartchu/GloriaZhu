Vital

2021

People use hands in daily life almost for everything. However, the existence of hands can be easily ignored.

We and our hands are inseparable. As a right-hander myself, I found my life suddenly got inconvenient when my left thumb was injured. It's even difficult to open a snack package. Thus, I realize how laborious hands are in daily life, and how it has been neglected by us.
