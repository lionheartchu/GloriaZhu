The Next Port

2025

Where will technological advancement drive us? I believe, utilizing new technologies like AI as our best assistants hits the future trends.

The Next Port reimagines autonomous delivery service in the setting of the low-altitude economy by integrating self-driving technology and drone logistics. The project aims to create an efficient drone-based delivery system to fulfill our future needs.

Research

The research explored emerging technologies in autonomous systems, drone capabilities, and future logistics needs. I have analyzed current delivery challenges and identified opportunities where low-altitude logistics could provide significant advantages.

Outcome

The Next Port combines ground-based autonomous hubs with aerial drone delivery to create a flexible, efficient logistics network that would operate efficiently in the future.
