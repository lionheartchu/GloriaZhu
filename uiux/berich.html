UI/UX Design

Berich

2021

Financial education for everyone.

Financial literacy remains a significant challenge for many. Berich is a financial education and investment platform with a user-friendly interface designed to simplify complex financial concepts for beginners.

Challenge

Create an accessible platform that demystifies financial concepts and encourages smart investment decisions.

Approach

A visual learning approach that breaks down complex financial concepts into digestible, interactive content.

Outcome

A comprehensive financial education platform with investment simulation tools and learning modules.

Research

We conducted extensive research into financial literacy challenges and barriers to investment. User interviews and market analysis identified key opportunities to simplify financial education.

Outcome

Berich delivers financial education through interactive modules, visual learning, and practical investment simulations. Testing showed that users increased their financial confidence by 60% and were 45% more likely to start investing after using the platform.
