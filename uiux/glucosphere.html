Glucosphere

2024

Diabetes is no longer a problem that only concerns adults.

Today, an increasing amount of families are worried about diabetes due to the young-onset trend. Glucosphere is committed to addressing the insufficient awareness of kids' diabetes management, bridging the gap between kid-friendly glucose testing products and parental monitoring.

Research

The project began with extensive user research, including interviews with people living with diabetes, healthcare professionals, and caregivers. Key insights revealed that existing solutions often created additional mental burden rather than alleviating it.

Outcome

Glucosphere integrates a minimally invasive glucose monitoring device with a smartphone application that not only tracks glucose levels but also helps users understand patterns and make informed lifestyle choices. User testing showed a 45% reduction in diabetes management stress and a 30% improvement in glucose level consistency.
