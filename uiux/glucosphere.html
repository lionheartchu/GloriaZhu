UI/UX Design Product Design · 2024

Gluco
Sphere

Kids-friendly glucose test kit & paired monitoring app for diabetic families.

Glucosphere bridges the gap between kid-friendly glucose testing and parental monitoring. It addresses the insufficient awareness of kids' diabetes management, making the medical process intuitive and less intimidating for the rising number of young patients.

01. Global Context

A Rising Trend

Diabetes is not just an adult issue anymore. With 537 million people affected worldwide and a projected 46% increase by 2045, the disease is spreading rapidly. China leads globally with over 140.9 million cases.

More alarmingly, the incidence among children under 20 years is rising steadily, transforming diabetes into a significant young-onset concern.

Youth Impact
1.21m

CHILDREN & ADOLESCENTS

Living with Type 1 diabetes globally, with cases increasing annually.

The Target
6-12

YEARS OLD

School-age children face unique challenges: puberty-induced insulin resistance and social stigma at school.

Why existing products fail kids?

My analysis of current market leaders like Care Touch and Abbott revealed a significant gap:

  • Scary Appearance: Tools look "rustic" and medical, frightening young users.
  • Painful Process: Fingersticks hurt, leading to resistance and crying.
  • Unclear Feedback: Kids can't interpret complex numbers on generic screens.
  • Social Stigma: Embarrassment prevents testing at school or with friends.
Design Opportunity

Gamified Health

How might we make kids check blood more spontaneously?

Key Goal: Transform the terrifying medical routine into a "game-like" task using a redesigned kit and a reward-based app ecosystem.

03. The Strategy

The Conflict

Fear & Forgetfulness

Medical devices look "sick" and scary. Kids avoid using them due to stigma at school and physical pain. Parents struggle to monitor compliance remotely.

The Solution

Gamified Ecosystem

A "Lunchbox-style" kit that hides the medical nature, paired with a gamified app that rewards consistency and enables real-time parent monitoring.

App: Gluardian

Enables parents to monitor results remotely, view historical trends, and send rewards to their kids.

Product: GlucoPal

A cute, box-like storage with redesigned lancing tools that are safe, kid-friendly, and convenient.

04. UI Design

The Gluardian App

The interface uses large, friendly typography and a reward system to encourage daily use. Data is visualized simply for kids, while parents get detailed analytics.

05. Product Effect

A Friend, Not a Device

The physical kit redefines the medical experience. It looks like a toy or school supply, reducing social anxiety when used in public.
