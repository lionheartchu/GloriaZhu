Glucosphere

2024

Diabetes is no longer a problem that only concerns adults.

Today, an increasing amount of families are worried about diabetes due to the young-onset trend. Glucosphere is committed to addressing the insufficient awareness of kids' diabetes management, bridging the gap between kid-friendly glucose testing products and parental monitoring.

Research

The project began with extensive research on young-onset diabetes today, and further conducted specific user research to determine the target user group. It is validated by the market research on the lack of product that are specifically designed for diabetic kids.
