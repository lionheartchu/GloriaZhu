Glucosphere

Diabetes is no longer just an adult problem.

Glucosphere bridges the gap between kid-friendly glucose testing and parental monitoring, making the medical process intuitive and less intimidating for the rising number of young patients.

Inspiration

Inspired by family history and rising trends in China, where diabetes is shifting from a "middle-age disease" to a serious young-onset concern.

The Trend

Cases among youth (< 20 years) are steadily increasing globally. Type 1 diabetes in children is seeing a constant annual rise.

The Challenge

Designing for school-age children (6-12) who face unique physical challenges like insulin resistance and social embarrassment at school.

Research

Inspired by family history and rising trends, where diabetes is shifting from a "middle-age disease" to a serious young-onset concern. Market research validated the target group as children aged 6–12, facing unique challenges like insulin resistance and social embarrassment at school.

Outcome

A cohesive health ecosystem: the Glucosphere Kit for painless testing and the Monitoring App for seamless data tracking. A "lunchbox-style" kit reduces stigma for kids, paired with a remote monitoring app for parents.
