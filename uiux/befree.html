UI/UX Design

Befree

2022

Finding balance in a digital world.

Today, our lives are increasingly dominated by screen time and digital commitments. Befree is a digital platform designed to help users break free from daily constraints and achieve better work-life balance through intuitive interface design.

Challenge

Design a digital platform that encourages healthier digital habits and helps users achieve work-life balance.

Approach

A user-centered design process focusing on minimalist interface design and behavior change psychology.

Outcome

An intuitive mobile application that helps users track and manage their digital wellbeing.

Research

The project began with research into digital wellbeing and work-life balance challenges. Interviews with potential users revealed common pain points around excessive screen time and difficulty disconnecting.

Outcome

Befree offers users a simple yet powerful way to track digital habits and gradually establish healthier patterns. User testing showed a 40% increase in awareness of digital habits and a 35% improvement in work-life balance satisfaction.
