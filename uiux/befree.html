Befree

Music tailored to every moment of your day.

Undisputedly, music occurs in scenes including studying, working, and exercising, and with relatively high frequency. Beyond being a great music player, Befree helps people pair different music styles with different emotions, mediums, and scenes, enabling people to enjoy music in a more convenient way.

Research

The research explored how people use music in different daily contexts and the challenges they face in finding the right music for each moment.

Outcome

A music app designed to help users enjoy music in multiple scenes freely and conveniently, with intuitive interface design that adapts to different usage contexts.
