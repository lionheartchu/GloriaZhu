Psychedelic Wonderland

2023

A journey through a surreal world.

Aligned to a similar storyline of Alice in Wonderland, this game enables the audience to experience a psychedelic environment from Alice in Wonderland by finding keys to escape from this world and return to reality.

Role

Coding, scene building, and integration

Team

Olivia Vue, Gloria Zhu, Matthew Chan, Ryan Zimmerman, Joey Bowser

Platform

macOS (Unity)

Overview

Psychedelic Wonderland is a first-person exploration game that plunges players into a surreal environment inspired by Alice in Wonderland. Players must navigate this dreamlike world to find keys that will allow them to escape and return to reality.
