Survivor “Simulator”

2023

Experience the world through nonhuman perspectives.

Survivor Simulator is about a virtual world with reversal laws of nature compared with the world we are living in, targeting letting the audiences think and feel from the perspective of nonhumans.

Process

Outcome

Survivor Simulator creates an immersive experience that challenges players to navigate a world where familiar rules no longer apply. The game has received positive feedback for its innovative approach to perspective-shifting and environmental storytelling.
